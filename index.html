    उत्तराखंड के चार धाम

    Also known as Devbhoomi or  Land of the Gods, Uttarakhand is home to numerous temples that welcome devotees all year round. Among the myriad religious institutions and circles attended by devotees  in Uttarakhand, the Char Dham Yatra is one of the most prominent. This yatra or pilgrimage is a tour around his four sacred sites of  Yamunotri, Gangotri, Kedarnath and Badrinath  in the Himalayan highlands. In Hindi 'char' means 4 and 'dham' means religious goal.
